Project Logic & Methodology This algorithm implements Dynamic Voltage and Frequency Scaling (DVFS) tailored for mobile and embedded devices. Unlike standard schedulers that prioritize finishing tasks as quickly as possible, this algorithm calculates the minimum frequency required to finish a task exactly by its deadline.

By reducing the operating frequency ($f$), the system can simultaneously reduce the supply voltage ($V$), leading to energy savings proportional to the square of the voltage reduction ($P \approx V^2f$).
Technical Features • Slack-Time Exploitation: Converts idle CPU cycles into energy savings.
• Power Budget Management: Enforces a strict Global Power Budget (GPB) to prevent thermal throttling and hardware damage.
• Predictive Scheduling: Dynamically adjusts the power profile based on task workload and remaining time-to-deadline.
